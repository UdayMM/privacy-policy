Delete Your Account

We respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. You can delete your Online Doctor Appointment account at any time using the in-app deletion feature.

Who Can Delete Their Account?

Patient Accounts: If you signed up as a patient using the app, you can delete your account directly through the app.

Doctor & Admin Accounts: These are business accounts created by healthcare organizations and cannot be self-deleted. Please contact your organization administrator for account management.

What Gets Deleted Immediately

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your patient profile (name, age, address)
  • Mobile number and authentication credentials
  • Notification tokens (FCM tokens) and preferences
  • App settings and saved preferences
  • Your ability to login to the app
  • Firebase Authentication account

Data Retained for Legal & Healthcare Compliance

Under healthcare regulations in India (Medical Council of India guidelines and hospital record-keeping laws), we are legally required to retain certain medical appointment records even after account deletion.

What Medical Records Are Retained:

  • Your appointment history with doctors
  • Your name and basic information in booking records
  • Visit dates and doctor consultation details
  • Payment records for appointments
  • Hospital and clinic visit logs

Why We Retain This Data:

Legal Requirement Authority Retention Period
Medical record-keeping Medical Council of India Regulations, 2002 7-10 years

Your Privacy is Protected:

  • You cannot be contacted through our app after account deletion
  • Your profile is deleted - you won't appear in patient searches
  • Booking records are accessible only to doctors/hospitals who treated you
  • Records cannot be linked back to your deleted account for marketing or other purposes
  • We do not use this data for any purpose except legal compliance and medical record-keeping
  • Data is encrypted at rest and in transit using industry-standard security

Re-creating Your Account

If you sign up again with the same mobile number after deletion:

  • No previous profile data is restored or accessible
  • You start with a clean slate
  • Previous appointment records remain with doctors/hospitals (legal requirement) but are not linked to your new account
  • You will need to re-enter all your information (name, age, address)

Legal Basis for Data Retention

We retain appointment records under the following legal authorities:

  • Medical Council of India (MCI) Professional Conduct Regulations, 2002
    Section 1.3.1: Requires doctors to maintain patient records for minimum 3 years (up to 10 years recommended)
  • Clinical Establishments (Registration and Regulation) Act, 2010
    Requires hospitals to maintain patient visit records for regulatory compliance
  • Indian Contract Act, 1872
    Contractual necessity to document service provision and consultation records
  • Information Technology Act, 2000 (as amended)
    Compliance with data protection and security requirements

Your Rights

Even after account deletion, you have the right to:

  • Request a copy of your retained appointment history (contact support)
  • Correct inaccuracies in medical records (contact the hospital/doctor directly)
  • Object to processing (limited - legal obligations take precedence)
  • Lodge a complaint with data protection authorities if you believe your rights are violated
  • Request explanation of why specific data is retained
